About us

Founded in 1956, Westview Theatre is one of the best and most efficient and luxurious theatres in all of Kent, and known for its services all through the UK and Ireland. Westview Theatre has hosted over 100 of UK's biggest Off-West End opening nights featuring celebrities and stars of the stage and screen.

Over the years, in addition to being home to several plays including but not limited to Hamilpun, The Lyon King and Wart Horse, Westview's stage has also had the honour of the performances of many big bands such as The Rolling Stoners, Queens and Pink Freud, who played their first mindblowing gig here in 1965.

We are dedicated to giving you the best possible theatrical experience, having always been at providing for our customers. Offering more than just popcorn and soft drinks, we offer a menus worth of food and drinks. We also offer our theatre as a venue for private hire. Our stage being ideal for events such as conferences, team building, product launches, as well as plays and shows of your own.
